'Tax Trek'



'Tax Trek' is a user-friendly income tax calculator app aimed at simplifying tax calculations for individuals, allowing them to understand their tax liabilities with ease.

Features:

User Input: Users can input essential information such as name, age, and total income. This data forms the basis of tax calculations, making the app easy for anyone to use.

Deduction Options: The app provides options to include deductions, helping users lower their taxable income. It includes a dedicated button that links directly to the official income tax website, ensuring users have access to up-to-date information on allowable deductions.

Tax Calculation: After entering the necessary information, the app calculates the total taxable income and provides an accurate tax amount. This feature saves users time and ensures accuracy.

Responsive UI: The app features an intuitive user interface designed in XML, optimized for a seamless experience across different Android devices.

Technology Stack:

Backend:

Developed entirely in Kotlin, which ensures efficient and reliable performance.

Frontend:

The UI is built with XML, adhering to modern design principles to create an engaging and easy-to-navigate user experience.
